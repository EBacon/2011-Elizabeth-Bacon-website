Elizabeth Bacon
Muse All Design

My creative and analytical capabilities became united in the professional practice of design. To discover the craft and develop my skills, I attended San Francisco State University's Master's program in the Department of Design and Industry. I also worked as a freelance Graphic Designer, Web Designer and Exhibit Designer from 1998 through 1999.

I enjoy the cross-disciplinary application of fundamental, problem-solving design processes in these various fields. As a freelance designer, I listened closely to my clients and developed solutions that efficiently met their needs and their budgets. Work ranged from web sites to visual identity work to exhibit graphics and structures.

My first professional design experience began in 1994, in Paris, France, where I co-invented a product design for storing compact discs in triangular binders. "Lazeryd" received a French design patent (to view, reload page then click left arrow once).
